About Us

We Are Feed My ChildrenLiberia

There is a great need for food in Liberia, Africa to help feed the less fortunate children. Due to the poor living condition and the lack of jobs of many parents in Liberia, so many kids are sleeping with hunger. Our team are on the field feeding the following kids:
a. Children without parents
b. Children selling on the streets.
c. Children from very poor family.
We want you to please help us care for those vulnerable kids.
With your support in contributing to this need, we can help reduce the hunger.
We just want to say a big thank you in advance for your support. No amount is too small. Please find attached photos.
You can also follow our page on Instagram and Facebook. Feed My Children-Liberia.
We would appreciate if you can help with us reach many kids by donating to our GofundMe page. Or our cashapp: $FeedmychildrenLib
